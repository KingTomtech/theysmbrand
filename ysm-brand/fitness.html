Fitness Tips

🥗

Nutrition Timing

Eat a balanced meal 2-3 hours before workouts. Post-workout, consume protein within 30 minutes for optimal recovery.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 3 liters of water daily. Proper hydration improves performance and aids in nutrient absorption.

😴

Prioritize Rest

Muscles grow during rest, not during workouts. Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night.

🎯

Consistency is Key

Small, consistent actions beat occasional perfection. Stick to your plan even when motivation wanes.
