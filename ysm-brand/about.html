Our Story

Proudly Zambian • Rooted in Wellness • Driven by Passion

Born in Lusaka

The YSM Brand was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a holistic wellness experience that celebrates the best of Zambia while embracing global standards of quality and luxury.

Based in the vibrant Northmead area of Lusaka, we've grown from a small passion project into a beloved lifestyle brand that touches lives through our three core divisions: Skincare, Culinary, and Fitness.

Every product we create, every meal we prepare, and every experience we curate is infused with our commitment to excellence and our deep love for our Zambian heritage.

Our Mission

To empower individuals on their wellness journey through premium, locally-crafted products and experiences that nourish the body, mind, and spirit.
